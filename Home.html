Where we are

 Jódar, our small town, is located in south-central province of Jaen, autonomous community of Andalusia. It has a privileged location, in the center of the southern half of the province, a step away from the World Heritage cities, Úbeda and Baeza; as well as the natural park of Sierra Mágina and the natural park of Cazorla, Segura and las Villas, declared this last Biosphere Reserve by UNESCO.
